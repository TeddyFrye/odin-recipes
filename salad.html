Salad by Teddy

Description

This salad is outta this world. It has everything you could ever need. Trust me.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Olives
  • Crutons
  • Olive Oil

These Are Your First Steps

  1. Toss Lettuce into a bowl.
  2. Halve Cherry Tomatoes and Olives.
  3. Toss above fixins into bowl with Lettuce, add Crutons, Olive Oil, and Cheese.
  4. And that's it, you're all done. It's that easy.
