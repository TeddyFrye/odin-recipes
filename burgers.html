Black Bean Burgers by Teddy

Description

These black bean burgers are outta this world. They have everything you could ever need. Trust me.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Ketchup
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Black Beans
  • 2 eggs
  • Optional: Chili powder, Garlic powder, Cumin, Paprika

These Are Your First Steps

  1. Cook Ground Beef/Refried Beans on stovetop over medium high heat.
  2. While that cooks, dice up Lettuce and prepare fixins.
  3. After a few minutes of cooking, (Once the Ground Beef is all brown) add Seasoning and 1 cup of Water. Cook and stir.
  4. Scoop contents of pan into individual Tortillas, top with the Hot Sauce, Cheese, Lettuce, and Sour Cream, in that order.
