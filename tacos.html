Description

These tacos are outta this world. They have everything you could ever need. Trust me.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Hot Sauce
  • Taco Seasoning
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Ground Beef
  • Veggie Substitute: Refried Beans

These Are Your First Steps

  1. Dry Black Beans in oven, laid out evenly on a pan. Do not overcook them.
  2. Mash up your now dried Black Beans in a bowl with the Ketchup, Eggs, Cheese, and Optional Seasonings.
  3. After they are properly mashed and mixed, (Mash them more than you think, but less than you hope.) Form 1/3 cup of the mixture into patties and place on parchment paper on a pan.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes on each side.
  5. Once done, place them on the Buns and serve with Lettuce and Tomato toppings.
